Mission

1 // Love

We are committed to acting out a positive role within the grappling community.

We encouarge all our members to be welcoming in their own clubs and towards other grapplers regardless of affiliation, rank or experience.

2 // Gather

We meet regularly to explore issues of faith, life and culture. We're a community of people who follow Jesus. We come from different backgrounds. We welcome all denominations and skeptics alike.

We encourage a healthy physical lifestyle and hold an open mat for all those who come along.

3 // Serve

We act out our mission in very practical ways be serving those in need, just like Jesus.

We train, support and fund projects for children living with poverty. Check out Akademi Kristus for more details.
