Mission

1 // Love

We are committed to acting out a positive role within the grappling community.

We encouarge all our supporters to be welcoming in their own clubs and towards other grapplers regardless of affiliation, rank or experience.

2 // Gather

We meet regularly to explore issues of faith, life and culture. We're a community of people who follow Jesus. We welcome all denominations and skeptics alike.

We encourage a healthy physical lifestyle and hold an open mat for all those who come along.

3 // Serve

We act out our mission in very practical ways be serving those in need, just like Jesus.
